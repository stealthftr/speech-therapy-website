About Lark

Hi, I'm Catherine — speech pathologist, and the person behind Lark.

For years I watched families go through the same frustrating maze: months of quiet worry, a long waitlist, a rushed appointment with whoever was free that day, and a report full of jargon nobody stopped to explain. Good kids and worried parents deserved better than a conveyor belt.

So I built Lark to be the opposite of a big, busy clinic — small enough that I truly know every child on my books, mobile so therapy happens where children are comfortable, and focused on one thing: children's speech. No rotating therapists. No sterile waiting rooms. No guessing what happened in the session. Just honest, expert, personal care.

How we work

Four promises we make to every family.

Honesty before enrolment

If your child doesn't need therapy, I'll tell you — on the free call, before you've spent anything. A practice that recommends therapy for every child isn't screening; it's selling.

Therapy where your child lives their life

At school or home, not a clinic that's foreign to them. Skills practised in real settings are skills that last.

You're always kept in the loop

Play-based sessions with clear goals — and, for Signature families, a note after every visit so you always know what we did and what to try at home.

No billing surprises

Funding options (including NDIS) explained up front, published fees, and a straight answer to "what will this cost?" before you commit.

Fifteen minutes. Honest answers. Zero pressure.

Tell me what you're seeing at home or at school — and let's work out whether it's typical, worth watching, or worth a closer look.
