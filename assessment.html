Why start with an assessment?

Because guessing is exhausting — and a clear picture changes everything.

An assessment isn't about finding what's "wrong." It's about understanding how your child communicates, what's getting in the way, and the fastest, kindest path forward.

You get real answers

Standardised and play-based tasks show us exactly where your child's speech and language are strong and where they need a hand — no more wondering.

You get evidence that opens doors

A detailed written report supports GP care plans, school and NDIS conversations, and funding requests. It has real value even if you never book therapy.

You get a plan you understand

We sit down together and explain it all in plain English, with clear next steps — including simple things to try at home, whatever you decide to do next.

What's included

Everything you need to move from worried to clear.

  • Pre-assessment questionnaireA short form about your worries, your child's history and your goals — so nothing gets missed.
  • A thorough assessment sessionStandardised measures plus relaxed, play-based tasks, led by a certified speech pathologist. Online by default; in-person available in Adelaide.
  • A plain-English written reportClear findings you can hand to your GP, your child's teacher, or the NDIS — no jargon wall.
  • A one-on-one feedback callWe walk you through what we found and answer every question. You'll leave understanding your child better.
  • A clear next-steps planSpecific recommendations and home strategies — even if that plan is "keep doing what you're doing and check back in a few months."

Our promise

We'll tell you the truth. If your child doesn't need therapy, we'll say so. If they'd benefit, we'll show you exactly why — and you decide, with no pressure. The plan is yours to keep, whether or not the next step includes us.

How the online assessment works

Done from your lounge room — where your child is at their best.

Assessment questions, answered

Do we have to do therapy with you afterwards?

Not at all. The report and plan are yours to keep and use however you like — including with another provider or your child's school. Many families use the assessment purely to get clarity and evidence.

Can the report be used for NDIS?

Yes — our reports are written to support NDIS planning and evidence requirements, as well as GP care plans and school conversations. We'll make sure it captures what you need.

How much does the assessment cost?

We'll give you the exact fee and walk through your funding options (including NDIS) on your free Discovery Call, so there are no surprises. See the pricing page for details.

How soon can we be seen?

Because assessments can be done online, we can usually offer a time within days rather than the months-long waits families often face elsewhere.
