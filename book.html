Before you book, you might be wondering…

Does my child need to be on the call?

No — this call is for you. It works well during naptime or after bedtime. If your child's chattering in the background, that's useful too, but it's not required.

Is this really free? What's the catch?

Really free, no catch. If an assessment or therapy makes sense, we'll explain the options and costs — and you decide, with zero pressure. If your child sounds on track, we'll happily say so.

What if I'm not sure anything's actually wrong?

"I'm not sure" is the most common reason parents call, and it's a great one. Fifteen minutes either gives you peace of mind or catches something early. Both are wins.
