Speech pathology at school

Therapy that comes to the classroom — less driving for you, more progress for them.

Lark delivers speech pathology inside Adelaide schools and kindies. For families, that means no more leaving work to ferry your child across town — and, with Signature sessions, an update email after every visit so you're never out of the loop. For schools, it means expert support with zero admin.

For parents

Your child gets expert therapy woven into their school day, in a place they already feel comfortable — and you keep your afternoons.

  • No driving, no waiting rooms, no missed work
  • An update email after every Signature visit — because you're not in the room, we make sure you're never in the dark
  • Skills practised in the real setting where your child needs them
  • We coordinate with your child's teacher, with your permission

Why school-based therapy works

Kids make faster progress where they actually use their words.

✓

Real-world practice

Skills learned at a clinic desk don't always travel. Skills learned at school — with real classmates, routines and pressures — stick.

✓

Less disruption, more consistency

No pulling your child out of the family's evening or your workday. Sessions happen while they're already at school.

✓

A team around your child

With your consent, we share simple strategies with teachers so support continues between our visits.
